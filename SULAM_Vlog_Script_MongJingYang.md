## DELIVERY NOTES

### Pacing Tips:
- **Opening:** Speak clearly but naturally, like you're telling a friend about your project
- **What I Did:** Pick up energy here - you're excited about the technical work
- **Challenges:** Slow down for the cultural reflection part - this is your "deep" moment
- **Growth & Impact:** Build emotion gradually, especially during the "grandparents' house" story
- **Closing:** Strong, confident finish - you've learned something important

### Visual Cues Summary:
1. **0:00-0:30** - You speaking (opening)
2. **0:30-1:15** - Website screenshots (categories, components, chatbox, quiz)
3. **1:15-2:00** - Back to you (challenges section)
4. **2:00-2:50** - Mix of you + website polish shots (growth section)
5. **2:50-3:30** - You speaking + final website b-roll (closing)

### Emotional Beats:
- Start: **Friendly, approachable**
- Middle: **Thoughtful, vulnerable** (when discussing cultural responsibility)
- Peak: **Genuinely moved** (grandparents' house moment)
- End: **Confident, purposeful**

### Camera Tips:
- Maintain eye contact during emotional moments
- Look at screen when "showing" technical work
- Natural hand gestures - don't overdo it
- Smile genuinely, especially at the beginning and end
